Installation

  1. From the Shopify Theme Store, click Try theme (free to preview) or Buy theme. Muffins is added to your theme library automatically.
  2. In your Shopify admin, go to Online Store → Themes. Muffins appears under Theme library.
  3. Click Customize to configure it, and Publish when you're ready to go live. Your previous theme is kept safely in the library.

Style presets

Muffins ships with three ready-made styles. To switch: in the theme editor, open Theme settings → Change theme style and pick a preset. Your content is preserved — only colors change.

Homepage sections

The homepage is fully sectioned — add, remove, and reorder anything in the theme editor. The default layout is:

  1. Hero banner — upload a wide lifestyle image (2400px+ recommended). Set eyebrow, heading, subheading, and button.
  2. Category highlights — the circular icon strip (details below).
  3. Featured collections — up to 4 occasion tiles. Assign a collection to each block; the tile uses the collection's featured image.
  4. Featured products — pick a collection (e.g. Bestsellers) and how many products to show. Quick-add buttons can be toggled on/off.
  5. Rich text — your brand story teaser.
  6. Testimonials — up to 6 review cards with star ratings.
Note: Until you assign real collections and product images, Muffins displays its built-in illustrated placeholders so your storefront never looks empty.

Category highlights strip

The signature circular icon row that guides shoppers to your key categories.

  1. In the theme editor, select the Category highlights section.
  2. Each circle is a block: set its Label, choose one of 10 built-in line-art icons (cake, mango, flowers, hamper, cookies, and more) or upload your own image.
  3. Link each block to a collection — or set a custom link (e.g. your café page).
  4. Add up to 12 blocks. On smaller screens the strip scrolls horizontally.

Collections, sorting & the dietary toggle

Collection pages include product count, sorting, pagination, quick-add, and a tag filter toggle — shown as "Eggless Only" by default.

Setting up the toggle

  1. Tag the relevant products (e.g. tag eggless) in Products → Tags.
  2. In the theme editor, open the collection page's Collection page section.
  3. Set Tag to filter by to your tag and Tag toggle label to the text shoppers see. Use any tag — vegan, gluten-free, sugar-free, etc.

Product pages

Product pages support multiple images, variant selection (updates price and availability live), quantity, and a full description area. For the best look, use square images of at least 1200×1200px with a consistent background style.

The About page template

  1. Go to Online Store → Pages → Add page and create your About page.
  2. In the Theme template dropdown on the right, select about.
  3. Customize the intro text, image-with-text story block, and contact strip in the theme editor.

FAQ

Why do I see illustrated cakes instead of my products?

Those are built-in placeholders. Assign real collections/products in each section and upload product images — your photos replace the placeholders automatically.

Can I change the announcement bar?

Yes — select the Announcement bar section in the header area of the theme editor and edit its text and link.
