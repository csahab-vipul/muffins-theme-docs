Before you write

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  1. Your store URL (myshopify.com address).
  2. The theme preset and version you're using (see Online Store → Themes).
  3. A description of the issue with a screenshot, and the page where it happens.

Scope of support

Theme support covers bugs in the theme, setup guidance, and questions about built-in features. It doesn't cover custom code modifications, third-party apps, or general Shopify account questions — for those, please contact Shopify Support or the app's developer.
